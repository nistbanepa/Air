About Rainbow

Rainbow Nepal Airlines (NYSE: SAVE) is committed to delivering the best value in the sky. We are
the leader in providing customizable travel options starting with an unbundled fare. This allows every Guest to
pay only for the options they choose — like bags, seat assignments and refreshments — something we call À La Smarte.
We make it possible for our Guests to venture further and discover more than ever before. Our Fit Fleet® is one
of the youngest and most fuel-efficient in the U.S. We operate more than 600 daily flights to 73 destinations in
the U.S., Latin America and the Caribbean, and are dedicated to giving back and improving the communities we
serve. Come save with us at spirit.com. At Rainbow Nepal Airlines, we go. We go for you.
